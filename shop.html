Our Farm Shop

Welcome to our farm shop website, where you can find all the information that you need about our products.

The farm shop has a wide range of fresh product and products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon.

We have free-range eggs that are laid by the chickens on our farm.

Our pork is reared in the fields around our house, giving it a distinctive flavour.

Choose from award winning pies with fillings such as: apricot or blackberry; hand-made chocolates; local honey and marmalade; homemade breads and cakes; locally sourced fruit juices, wines, beers and ciders.

We also sell fresh vegetables grown in our garden!

We also have a small café attached to the farm shop so people can enjoy their purchases immediately.

Our café serves a selection of hot and cold drinks, cakes, pastries, and light lunches, all made with fresh, local ingredients.

Some of the most popular items in a farm shop are: fresh eggs, milk, yogurt, cheese, bread, and cakes.

Our award-winning range of jams contain no artificial colourings or preservatives and are available in a variety of flavours.

Our pies are freshly made daily using the best locally sourced ingredients and come in a variety of flavours including meat, vegetable and fruit.

A variety of seasonal, fresh, organic vegetables grown on our farm are available to purchase.

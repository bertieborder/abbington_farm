Explore the Fun at Abbington Farm Park

At Abbington Farm, there's something for everyone to enjoy — whether you're looking for a relaxing day outdoors or hands-on farm fun, our farm park offers a wide variety of engaging activities for visitors of all ages.

From classic hayrides through the fields to pumpkin picking in the autumn and getting lost in our seasonal corn maze, there's always an adventure waiting. Each activity is designed to bring families closer to nature and provide a true taste of farm life.

In addition to our seasonal highlights, you can also:

  • Meet our friendly farm animals and observe how they live in well-kept, natural enclosures designed to reflect their natural habitat.
  • Get hands-on with real farm tools — try out a pitchfork, shovel, or wheelbarrow and learn how farmers use them every day.
  • Pick your own produce straight from our vegetable garden — a perfect way to bring home fresh, local ingredients and connect with where your food comes from.

Whether you're a curious child, a nature-loving adult, or just looking for a peaceful day out, Abbington Farm Park is a wholesome and memorable experience.

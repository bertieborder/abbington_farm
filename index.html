Nestled in the scenic countryside between Edinburgh and Carlisle, Abbington Farm is the perfect stop for families, garden lovers, and curious travellers alike.

Once a traditional farm, Abbington has been lovingly transformed into a vibrant visitor attraction, offering something for everyone. Meet our friendly animals in the petting zoo, let the kids burn off energy in the outdoor Play Park, or enjoy a relaxing stroll through our peaceful walking trails.

Pop into our charming farm shop for fresh local produce, artisan treats, and beautiful plants, or unwind in our cosy caf√©, serving home-baked goodies and locally roasted coffee.

Whether you're planning a family day out, a garden-focused getaway, or just looking for a quality spot to stretch your legs, Abbington Farm is a warm, welcoming destination for all.
